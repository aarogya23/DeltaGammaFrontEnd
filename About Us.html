About Us

Welcome to Delta Gamma Academy, we are dedicated to helping students prepare for the Institute of Engineering (IOE) entrance examinations. Our primary mission is to equip students with the essential knowledge and skills needed to excel in their engineering entrance exams.

We have a team of experienced and qualified instructors who are committed to providing high-quality education and personalized guidance. Our focus is on creating a supportive and engaging learning environment that fosters academic excellence and prepares our students to succeed in their future engineering careers.

Delta Gamma Academy was founded with the vision of empowering students to achieve their dreams of pursuing engineering through the Institute of Engineering (IOE) entrance examinations. Our primary mission is to equip students with the essential knowledge and skills needed to excel in these competitive exams.

Since our inception, we've built a team of experienced and qualified instructors dedicated to providing high-quality education and personalized guidance. Our focus is on creating a supportive and engaging learning environment that fosters academic excellence and prepares our students to succeed in their future engineering careers.
